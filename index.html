Services

Statistical Research

I help clients understand their data using Quantitative Methods. The advantage of measuring and quantitavely testing hypotheses about a business, a market, prices or customer behavior, is that it provides management with a scientific approach to decision making, improving understanding while reducing error.


Building Analytics

In case the data exists within the company, I can build Analytics solutions that communicate relevant aspects of the data to management and stakeholders; in case the data does not, I can help devise applications that source it externally before any Analytics are prototyped. Properly done, Analytics improve productivity.

About

  • 2014 - 2015

    Founder of DUE Arbre AB

    Lund, Sweden

    After a Ph.D. in Economics, I decided that the best way for me to help people and clients was to set up my own consulting firm. It allows me to combine my passions for statistics, tech and business.

  • 2013 - 2014

    Authored Book: Real Estate and Banks

    The book came about as a result of teaching mainstream macroeconomics during my PhD studies (which coincidentally does not offer any scientific explanations for the emergence of housing bubbles). The book accounts for the financing provided by commercial banks to the Swedish real estate market in the form of covered bonds, with the central bank as an implied guarantor for the banks' liabilities. Only available in Swedish.

  • 2009 - 2014

    Doctor of Philosophy in Economics

    Lund University, Sweden

    The education culminated in a dissertation with the title Empirical Essays in Expectations. Chapter 3 establishes that the communication channel of the Swedish Central Bank (Riksbanken) exhibits small and transient effects as a means of managing Inflation Expectations.

    The lion's share of course work consisted of studies in statistics and econometrics, which I also lectured in.

  • 2006 - 2009

    Mergers & Acquisitions Analyst

    Bloomberg, London, United Kingdom

    Working as an M&A analyst for a global finance/tech company during the height of the financial crisis was a very interesting, educative and a challenging experience. The connectedness of individuals through information, and companies through leverage, had a striking effect on the macroeconomic environment.

  • 2002 - 2006

    M.Sc. in International Economics

    Lund University, Sweden

    Université Dauphine, France

    The education culminated in a master thesis with the title What is the Probability of a Recession in the United States? It came in handy during the financial crisis of 2008.

  • 1997 - 1998

    Military Service

    Coastal Artillery KA3 & KA4, Sweden

    My military service as a Communications Staff Officer (Sgt) provided me with a technical understanding of radio, network and computer hardware, as well as leadership training.
