Data Consulting Services

Our Services

We are entrepreneurial data professionals with a deep understanding of all layers that go into an Analytics/BI solution, from presentation to how statistical models interact with programming and data models in application and back end systems. Our experiences range from project leading the adaptation of real time information systems, to construction, validation and deployment of statistical models.

In our professional consulting engagements, we seek to add value by making sure that we:
- understand what the client wants and how to map those demands into requirements and objectives,
- analyze risks and constraints to those objectives, and proactively develop feasible road maps,
- achieve the objectives by acting agile, sharing information with stakeholders, responding to feedback, and being hands on.

    The book accounts for the financing provided by commercial banks to the Swedish real estate market in the form of covered bonds, with the central bank as an implied guarantor for the banks' liabilities.
